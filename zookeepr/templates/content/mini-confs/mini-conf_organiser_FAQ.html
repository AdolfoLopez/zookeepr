Important restrictions

Part of linux.conf.au

Mini-confs run as part of the linux.conf.au conference programme. Thus, when submitting a miniconference you agree that if accepted, you will organise your miniconference to suit the venue and timetable provided by the linux.conf.au organisers. We particularly ask that all miniconferences observe the timetable provided by organisers, so that attendees can move between mini-confs.

Sponsorship

Please do not separately organise sponsorship for your mini-conf. If you think there would be sponsor interest in your miniconference, please contact our sponsorship team at sponsors@mel8ourne.org, who will then discuss with you appropriate arrangements and act as a single contact point for the sponsors. Do not approach sponsors directly, and if you receive direct inquiries, re-direct them to sponsors@mel8ourne.org or the Sponsors page. Failure to do this may jeopardise linux.conf.au's relationship with all sponsors, and may result in your miniconference being cancelled.

Advertising

Due to advertising restrictions with our sponsors, your mini-conf may not display or distribute banners, posters, leaflets or other advertising material without prior written agreement from the linux.conf.au organising team. You may organise the design and sale of shirts or clothing provided that this clothing is specific to your miniconf (eg "so-and-so miniconf" not "so-and-so project").
