Marvellous Melbourne

Australia's 2nd largest city is located on the south coast of Victoria. Situated on the Delta of the Yarra and Maribyrnong rivers, and perched on Port Phillip Bay, Melbourne is a great city in which to come and play. It is surrounded by parks, filled with some of the best retail the country has to offer, and home to a dazzling array of things to do.

Food

Melbourne is the culinary capital of Australia. The freshness and diversity of cuisines available should satisfy everyone's tastes and budget.

Travel

Melbourne is serviced by planes, trains, boats and automobiles. For all your travel information, have a look at Wikitravel.
